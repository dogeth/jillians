p(title). Welcome to Jillian’s!


p. The boutique for the young at heart, and has an eye for quality and detail.


p. Jillian’s has an invigorated approach to fashion and a commitment to the mature market.


p. Our clothes are a blend of old favourites and new classics mixed with Jewellery, Handbags and accessories.  Designers include the old classics like Black Pepper, Toronto, FM, Jaki- K, Jodanne, Lucia Darlin and Pelaco just to name a few.


p. Jillians specialize in mature fashions, Mother of the bride, evening and casual wear with an extensive size range from 8-26.

p. The next chapter in the evolution of Jillian’s will be the website development with monthly specials and a mail order facility for our country &amp; international customers.


p. Our Shop is open Monday to Friday 9am to 5.00pm and 9am to 2pm on Saturdays so why not drop in to the store, say hello, grab a bargain and ask us about our VIP programme.
